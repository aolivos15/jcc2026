The International Conference of the Chilean Computer Science Society (SCCC) is pleased to invite the academic and professional community to participate in the Chilean Computer Science Days (JCC) 2026. This event provides a platform for the presentation of research work, exchange of ideas, and strengthening of collaboration in the field of computing in Chile and internationally. The SCCC conference serves as a forum for presenting recent, original work that has not been previously published in all areas of Computer Science.

Paper Submission

  • Use the IEEE format.
  • Papers may be written in Spanish, English, or Portuguese and should be between 4 and 8 pages, depending on the type of contribution.
  • Articles must be submitted through Microsoft's Conference Management Toolkit.
  • By submitting a paper, authors implicitly accept that at least one author must be registered and present the paper at the conference. Only articles that have been presented by their authors during the conference, and that comply with the IEEE quality review standards, will be published in the proceedings.

CMT acknowledgment: The Microsoft CMT service was used for managing the peer-reviewing process for this conference. This service was provided for free by Microsoft and they bore all expenses, including costs for Azure cloud services as well as for software development and support.
